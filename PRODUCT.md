# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The primary users are people who want a small, personal Windows desktop companion rather than a conventional productivity suite. They are likely office workers, e-commerce operators, support staff, or friends of the author who need quick access to daily plans, reading, temporary clipboard collection, countdowns, account notes, and lightweight personalization while working across many desktop windows.

## Product Purpose

Dynamic Island is a Windows desktop floating island for lightweight personal productivity and mood. It keeps tiny, frequently used tools close without becoming a full-screen app: daily plan, work countdown, novel reading, photo carousel, temporary clipboard, calculator, account vault, file naming, settings, and personalization. Success means the island feels quick, calm, useful, and non-disruptive during normal computer work.

## Brand Personality

Clean, gentle, personal. The intended feeling is Apple-like restraint: rounded, calm, polished, light in motion, and easy to trust. Personalization and cute themes are allowed, but they should support delight without making the interface feel noisy or childish.

## Anti-references

This should not look like a generic enterprise admin panel, CRM dashboard, or internal system page. It should also avoid heavy decorative glass, excessive sticker clutter, slow animations, and visual effects that hurt readability or performance.

## Design Principles

1. Stay small until the user asks for more.
2. Make the current task obvious within one glance.
3. Use personalization as accent, not visual noise.
4. Favor fast, quiet motion over dramatic animation.
5. Keep desktop work uninterrupted; the island should help, not compete.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for practical WCAG AA readability where possible, especially for text over translucent and wallpaper backgrounds. Support reduced-motion users by avoiding mandatory heavy animation. Do not rely only on color or hover states for critical actions. Controls should remain readable on both dark and personalized themes.
